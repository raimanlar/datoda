Online Dating: 6 tips that will help you get acquainted effectively

In winter, loneliness is especially noticeable. I want to hug a loved one, watch Christmas movies with my face buried in my own shoulder, dress up the tree, buy gifts and enjoy it together taste of tangerines.

We have collected 6 practical tips that will help you spend time on a Dating site as efficiently as possible.

Fix your mistakes and find your soul mate faster!

1. Choose the right Dating site

There are a lot of Dating apps and sites, but not all of them suitable for your goals. There are services where a certain contingent gathers or sites for flirting and sex Dating.

You don't need to register on many sites at once. Just choose 1-2 sites that you trust and have good reviews about. The RusDate app, for example, is designed specifically for serious users Dating sites with Russian-speaking users. Try it, just in case fate has been looking for you on RusDate for a long time.

2. Suitable photo

A lot also depends on the choice of a photo for the questionnaire. Let it be a simple portrait photo. Without sunglasses, palm trees, expensive cars or alcohol. You would write to a person who has a photo on the main page with a glass of beer or he is next to an expensive Bentley (probably someone else's). Many will pass by. So don't try to be better than you really are. Show your true self.

3. Tell us about yourself

Users skip the questionnaire completion stage and start Dating right away. This is a big mistake. Show that you are serious: fill out the form and tell us about yourself. Feel free to write about your Hobbies, travels, and who you would like to find. Write whatever you think is important and try to avoid it common phrase. It will be much easier for a user who reads your profile to find something common and start a conversation.

4. Think about who you want to find

Decide which men (or women) you are interested in. This way you can narrow your search circle and save time those who are obviously not your type. Don't look for your dream partner. Communicate with interesting, likable people. It is important that the interlocutor is ready to meet in the near future.

5. Pay attention to communication

If the dialog becomes everything more interesting and captures you two, go to the real world. But if the correspondence has turned into an exchange of monosyllabic phrases, you should not continue communicating. If you don't understand the other person's jokes and they don't write correctly, it's unlikely that you will be able to match each other. If the other person sends you intimate photos, block them immediately and notify the service supports. Communication should be built with adequate, pleasant and interesting people.

6. Meeting

The main purpose of communication is to meet in the real world. Don't drag out your correspondence for months. Make an appointment after a week or two of communication. You will immediately understand whether it is worth it to continue the acquaintance.

It is possible to build a serious relationship by simply meeting someone online. It is only necessary to make a little effort, approach the Dating process correctly, know what you want, and be patient. You will definitely succeed! ;)
