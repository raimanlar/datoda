10 secrets of successful Dating

10 secrets of successful Dating

There are no universal ways to get acquainted and find your man. The main thing is to be confident and know what you want. But there are techniques that work better than others. Today we will tell you about 10 secrets of successful Acquaintances. The better you get to know yourself , the easier it will be to get to know someone who is likable.

1. Be open and open-minded

Don't shut yourself up, be open and welcoming. Be interested in the people you meet and meet online. You never know what the next meeting will turn into.

2. Before you meet others, get to know yourself

Get to know yourself. Think about what you like, what is interesting, what you love in your life. Think about and write down what you would like to know about the other person. When you know yourself, you will be able to understand who you want to see next to you. This is a simple but important exercise on the way to success. successful Dating.

3. Look matters

During Dating or a first date in real life (if you met each other online), eye contact is important. Many people find it difficult to look people they don't know in the eye. Others, on the contrary, stare intently they look into the eyes of the other person, thus embarrassing and making the other person feel uncomfortable. Find a middle ground between being shy and looking too closely.

4. If your eyes have crossed, move on to getting to know each other

Only for Dating in real life. If your eyes meet, try it start a conversation. Be polite. Immediately ask the person if they don't mind communicating. Don't be afraid to get rejected, and don't be disappointed if you didn't get to start a conversation the first time.

5. Be open to the extent of

On the first date it is not worth telling something you'll regret later. Communicate on neutral topics, make jokes, ask questions about Hobbies, movies, books, and travel. You do not need to immediately dump all aspects of life on the interlocutor. If a certain question seems too personal or unpleasant, say so directly.

6. Show your best self in the first few seconds Acquaintances

Psychologists claim that we make an opinion about whether we like someone or not in the first 5 seconds of a meeting. Show yourself on your best side. No, you don't have to pretend, just be yourself. The main thing is openness, goodwill, and positive attitude. A smile and pleasant conversation are the key success's.

7. Less expectations

You don't have to expect anything from the date or the other person. The more imaginative, the more frustrating. Because of the expectations and the invented image, you may not see the real person.

8. Stick balance in the conversation

Take care not to talk only about yourself. Take an interest in the other person and maintain a dialogue. The date should not resemble a monologue or interview.

9. Don't rush to give advice and solve other people's problems

If the interlocutor complains about something, you do not need to rush to give advice or immediately offer help. Remember, this is only the first meeting. Probably, the person wants only to share experiences. It is enough to ask politely: "Do you need advice or do you just want to share it with me?"

10. Pay attention to the fact that feel close to a new acquaintance

Well, if on the first date there was chemistry, felt attracted to each other. But that's not enough to tell you it's your man. Pay attention to how you feel, how well and calmly near the object of sympathy. Discuss each other's goals and interests, to understand whether it is worth continuing the acquaintance, whether there are prospects for this communication.

Allow yourself to worry during the introduction and the first meeting. Being shy and timid at first is quite normal. Most likely, the other person is just as worried as you are. Be self, falseness and play are always felt.

Don't stop getting acquainted and looking for your person. Sooner or later, you'll meet the one you've been looking for. Good Dating!
